3.5 Test Accounts

The steering committee grants SMALL project accounts with restricted resources before MIDDLE and LARGE projects are finally reviewed. Those test accounts are granted for any project proposal that comes with a sufficiently explanatory abstract and/or detailed description.

However, the risk of useless work in case the project is rejected remains with the project proposer.

The duration of a test project is at most one year.
